## Baseline commitments (independent of visual direction)
- Accessibility: WCAG AA contrast for all UI text and controls; all functionality
  keyboard-operable; form controls labeled; palette colors always paired with hex
  text, never conveyed by swatch alone.
- The original image and pattern preview sit side-by-side (README).
- Layout stays usable on a laptop screen without horizontal scrolling.
